How to Make a Website in Nepal That Actually Drives Sales

June 27, 2026 • 8 min read • by TrexoByte Editorial
HIGH-CONVERTING WEBSITES IN NEPAL

If you are looking to make a website in Nepal for your business, you might be tempted to call up a developer, pick a generic template, write a few descriptions, and host it. But here is the hard truth: 92% of business websites in Nepal fail to generate a single sales inquiry. They function as digital brochures, collecting virtual dust on server hosting racks.

A website shouldn't be an expense; it should be your highest-performing sales rep. When built with a strategy-first mindset, it builds trust, optimizes your localized digital reach, and automatically captures leads. Here is the step-by-step roadmap to building a web presence in Nepal that translates directly into business revenue.

1. The Foundation: Securing a Free .com.np Domain

Before launching, you need a domain name. In Nepal, you can register a free .com.np domain through Mercantile Communications (via register.com.np). This is highly advantageous because search engine algorithms heavily prioritize localized country-code domains (ccTLDs) for queries initiated within Nepal.

To secure a business .com.np domain, you must submit scanned copies of:

  • Company Registration Certificate: Official authorization from the Office of the Company Registrar (OCR) or Ward office.
  • PAN/VAT Registration Document: Necessary for proof of tax compliance.
  • Official Request Letter: A signed, stamped cover letter on company letterhead requesting the domain name.

Ensure the domain name aligns exactly with your registered business name to prevent administrative rejection.

2. Design for Nepal's Mobile-First Audience

According to the latest telecommunication reports, over 87% of internet users in Nepal access websites via smartphones. If your website is designed primarily for a desktop display, you are throwing away almost your entire market. Mobile responsiveness is no longer optional; it is the absolute standard.

"A premium user experience on mobile devices isn't about fitting a desktop layout into a small screen. It is about restructuring interactions for touch-scroll navigation, optimizing media for low bandwidth networks, and fast loading."

To optimize for mobile, prioritize lightweight coding patterns, responsive fluid grids, and touch-optimized buttons that prevent tapping errors (which is especially critical on iOS devices).

3. Integrate Local Payment Gateways: eSewa & Khalti

An e-commerce website cannot drive sales if checkout paths are cluttered with friction. While credit card processing is gaining ground, the vast majority of digital transactions in Kathmandu, Pokhara, and across Nepal are powered by digital wallets.

Integrating local API gateways like eSewa and Khalti allows customers to scan QR codes or pay directly using their wallet accounts. This builds instant trust. Always ensure your payment processes are secured with SSL certificates and clear success redirect messages.

4. Authority Local SEO & Generative Engine Optimization (GEO)

How will customers find you? Simply having a site won't rank you on Google or display your answers inside AI engines like ChatGPT, Claude, or Gemini. You must optimize your content for both search systems (SEO) and answer systems (AEO/GEO):

  • Local Intent Keywords: Target specific local phrases such as "best branding agency Kathmandu" or "e-commerce web design services in Nepal" rather than generic international terms.
  • Direct Answer Formats: AI engines fetch direct answers. Structure your sub-headings clearly and provide concise, bold, factual statements answering target questions directly.
  • Factual Citations: Cite recognized local entities (such as Nepal Rastra Bank for financial services, or Mercantile for domains) to feed generative models with highly authoritative context.

Quick Facts: Web Design in Nepal (AEO/GEO Section)

Q: How much does web design cost in Nepal?

A: Website development prices vary. Standard responsive sites range from Rs. 18,000 to Rs. 40,000, while premium custom web designs with advanced SEO systems, speed optimization, and payment gateways range from Rs. 60,000 to Rs. 150,000+.

Q: What is needed to accept online payments on a website in Nepal?

A: You need to integrate APIs from local digital payment providers like eSewa, Khalti, or commercial bank payment APIs. This requires setting up developer merchant accounts with your business registration documents.

Q: Can I set up a website on local Nepali hosting?

A: Yes, there are local hosting providers. However, for optimum speeds and uptime, it is recommended to use cloud infrastructure platforms (like Cloudflare, AWS, or premium international SSD servers) that utilize local Content Delivery Networks (CDNs) to load content instantly in Nepal.

5. Focus on the Conversion Action

Every page on your site must guide users toward a single clear outcome. Whether it's "Start a Project", "Call Now", or "Message on WhatsApp", your call to action (CTA) must be impossible to miss. Keep your forms simple, ask only for essential information (Name, Email, Phone), and respond within minutes. Speed to lead is the ultimate secret to digital agency conversion.
